Pierre Perrault

I am a deep learning research engineer at Idemia, where I work on computer vision for video understanding. I received my PhD from ENS Paris-Saclay and Inria Lille in November 2020, advised by Michal Valko (DeepMind) and Vianney Perchet (CREST, ENSAE).
